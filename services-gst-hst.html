Input Tax Credits

Claim every eligible credit — with airtight records.

As a registered business, you can claim credits for HST paid on business inputs, reducing your overall tax burden. We ensure you claim all eligible credits while meeting CRA's strict documentation requirements.

Eligible Input Tax Credits

Credits apply to HST paid on goods and services used to make taxable supplies. Understanding which expenses qualify is critical for claiming maximum credits and avoiding over-claims.

  • • Professional services and consulting
