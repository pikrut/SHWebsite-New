The First Decision

Federal vs. provincial incorporation.

We guide you through this decision based on your business scope, growth plans, and operational needs.

Federal Incorporation

Creates a corporation operating throughout Canada and internationally — ideal for businesses planning expansion beyond Ontario.

  • ✓ Nationwide and international operating authority
  • ✓ Name protection across Canada
  • ✓ Continuous corporate existence
  • ✓ Mobility between provinces

Provincial Incorporation

Creates a corporation operating under Ontario law — ideal for businesses primarily operating in Ontario, simpler and more cost-effective.

  • ✓ Lower incorporation costs
  • ✓ Simpler annual compliance requirements
  • ✓ Ontario name protection
  • ✓ Familiar provincial legislation

Choosing the Right Structure

Incorporation is one of several options.

We help you understand all options and choose the structure that best suits your objectives, tax situation, and risk management needs.

01

Sole Proprietorship

Simplest structure where you are the business — simple registration, lower costs, full personal liability, T1 income reporting.

02

Partnership

Two or more individuals sharing ownership — shared ownership, partnership agreement, joint liability, flow-through taxation.

03

Incorporation

Separate legal entity — limited liability, perpetual existence, more complex compliance, tax planning options.
