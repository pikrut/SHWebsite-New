Most corporations have six months from the end of their fiscal year to file a T2 corporate tax return. We ensure your filing meets the appropriate deadline and takes advantage of any available extensions if needed for tax planning purposes.
Salary reduces corporate taxable income while generating CPP contributions and personal income tax. Dividends are paid from after-tax corporate income and may have different personal tax implications. The optimal approach depends on your personal tax bracket, business needs, and retirement planning.
To qualify, your corporation must be a Canadian-controlled private corporation with active business income. The deduction applies to the first $500,000 (approximately) of active business income. We assess your eligibility and ensure you maximize this valuable deduction.
Integration is the Canadian tax principle that aims to equalize the combined corporate and personal tax on business income whether extracted as salary or dividend. Integration is imperfect, creating planning opportunities we leverage to optimize your situation.
